Hi, I’m Ania.

I am an experienced e-learning course developer. I combine strong technical skills with a creative approach and a solid understanding of e-learning best practices, which enables me to design courses that genuinely support learners’ development as well as the goals of the organizations I work with. I believe that well-designed e-learning not only delivers knowledge but also inspires action and makes learning easier to apply in practice.
