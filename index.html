Hi, I’m Ania.

I am an experienced e-learning course developer. I specialize in creating interactive and engaging educational content, mainly using tools such as Articulate Storyline 360, Canva, Vyond, and DaVinci Resolve. These tools allow me to create modern, visually consistent, and engaging learning materials—from interactive modules to animations and video content.

I combine strong technical skills with a creative approach and a solid understanding of e-learning best practices, which enables me to design courses that genuinely support learners’ development as well as the goals of the organizations I work with. I believe that well-designed e-learning not only delivers knowledge but also inspires action and makes learning easier to apply in practice.
